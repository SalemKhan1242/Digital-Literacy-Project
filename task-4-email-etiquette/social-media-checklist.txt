# Do’s and Don’ts of Social Media

## DO's
- Think before posting and consider the long-term impact of your content.  
- Protect your privacy by using strong passwords and enabling two-factor authentication.  
- Verify information before sharing it publicly to avoid spreading misinformation.  
- Be respectful and professional while communicating with others online.  
- Use social media to learn new skills, network, and grow academically.  
- Maintain a positive and consistent digital identity across platforms.  

## DON’Ts
- Don’t share personal or sensitive information (e.g., passwords, address, phone number).  
- Don’t engage in cyberbullying, trolling, or online arguments.  
- Don’t post offensive, inappropriate, or harmful content.  
- Don’t spread fake news or forward unverified information.  
- Don’t share your real-time location publicly for safety reasons.  
- Don’t become addicted—limit screen time and avoid excessive usage.
